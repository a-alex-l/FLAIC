Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025.07.29

Welcome to FLAIC. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information...

1. Information We Do Not Collect

We do not collect or store any personal information on our servers. Your API key for the Gemini service is stored exclusively in your browser's local storage with your consent and is never sent to our servers.

2. Information Stored on Your Browser

With your explicit consent, we will store your API key in your browser's local storage. This is done solely for your convenience so that you do not have to re-enter the key on subsequent visits. You can revoke this consent at any time by clearing your browser's local storage for this site.
